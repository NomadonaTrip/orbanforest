Common questions

Do I need special equipment?

Any modern iPhone or Android.

Can I still sell on Amazon?

Yes. No exclusivity. We're an additional channel, not a replacement.

What does it cost authors?

$49 one-time. Includes free creation tokens. No monthly fees.

Who owns the content I create?

You do.

Is this safe for children?

Yes. The parent controls the device and chooses the mode. Listen mode uses zero screen visuals. No ads, no social features, no data collected from children without parental consent.

How is this different from Disney's AR books?

Disney defaulted to full animation — passive consumption that replaced imagination. We built three modes so the reader controls how much the technology reveals. Listen mode is what Disney never built and what educators always wanted.
