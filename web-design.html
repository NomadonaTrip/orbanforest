Your website is the one piece of your business that's working — or should be — twenty-four hours a day. If it isn't pulling its weight, it isn't the internet's fault. It's the design, the copy, the speed, and the fact that nobody connected it to the rest of your business.

Custom Web Design, Not Templates

"Custom website design" gets used loosely. Here's what we mean by it: we start from your business — your actual customers, your actual pricing, your actual competitors in Ontario — and design the site for how those people buy. Not from a Figma template that looks the same as every other small business site in the province.

The difference shows up in two places. First, in conversion rate: template sites tend to bury the thing the visitor actually wants. Second, in search: a custom site lets us write for the specific phrases your customers are typing, and structure the pages so Google understands what you do.

Web Design for Small Business — What You Actually Need

Most small business websites in Ontario are doing one job: describing the business. That's the bare minimum. A site that earns its cost does more:

  • Captures leads before they leave. Forms, chat, direct booking — whatever fits your buying cycle. Not buried in a "Contact" page nobody finds.
  • Loads fast. Under two seconds on a phone with 3G. Anything slower and Google starts ranking you lower on top of losing visitors.
  • Ranks locally. "Plumber Brantford," "restaurant near me," "dentist Cambridge" — the phrases that bring paying customers in, not the brand-name keywords that sound impressive but convert nothing.
  • Is easy to update. You shouldn't need to call us to change a phone number or add a promotion. We hand over full editing access and show you how.
  • Hooks into the rest of your business. Every form submission should land in your CRM, inbox, and (if you want it) an automated follow-up sequence. Disconnected websites waste most of the leads they earn.
