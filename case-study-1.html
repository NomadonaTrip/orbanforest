Loewen Property Works

Loewen Property Works is a full-service property maintenance company based in Ontario, serving residential and commercial clients. When they came to us, their website had been down for months — and with it, their visibility online. We designed a responsive website that restored their digital presence and built credibility with clients.

The Challenge

Months offline in an increasingly digital market

When Loewen Property Works came to us, their website had been down for months. The longer it stayed offline, the more ground they lost — search rankings slipped, potential clients couldn't find them, and competitors filled the gap.

Without a working online presence, Loewen was losing credibility with the very clients searching for their services.

Competitor analysis screenshot — search landscape
Our Approach

Responsive, credible, and built to convert — designed to restore trust

We designed a clean, responsive website that immediately communicates trust, professionalism, and the full scope of Loewen's services. Every page was built to affirm the brand and guide visitors toward a single goal: booking a job.

  • Streamlined navigation with prominent CTAs on every page
  • Service pages optimized for local SEO with geo-targeted keywords
  • Fast load times under 0.5 seconds on mobile networks
  • Responsive design tested across 12+ device configurations
  • Google Business Profile integration for local map pack visibility

The Results

From months of downtime to a lead-generating machine

With a professional website back online, customers searching for Loewen Property Works' services could finally engage with the brand. Visitors arrived already acquainted with the company's promise — helping Loewen close more jobs and rebuild the credibility that months of downtime had eroded.
